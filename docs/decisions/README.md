# decisions

Architectural Decision Records (ADRs) — one significant decision per file, with
the context and consequences that make it make sense later.

- **Filename**: `NNNN-short-slug.md` (zero-padded, sequential: `0001-`, `0002-`).
- **Status**: `Proposed`, `Accepted`, `Superseded by NNNN`, or `Deprecated`.
- Keep the record even when superseded — supersede, don't delete. History is the
  point.

## Template

```markdown
# NNNN. Title

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What forces are at play? What problem or constraint prompted this?

## Decision

What we decided, stated plainly.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, harder, or newly required as a result. Trade-offs accepted.
```
